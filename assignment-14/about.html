About Us

 

 

The Big Three

Lookout Ski Pass, Lost Trail Ski Area, and Montana Snowbowl the main three ski resorts available to Missoula County residents. Lookout Ski Pass is located less than two hours down I-90 headed from Missoula. Lost Trail Ski Area is just a few hours down Highway-12 past Hamilton. Montana Snowbowl is in Missoula, located less than 30 minutes from downtown. Each mountain offers a variety of terrain and each receives different kinds of snow-fall throughout the year. Needless to say, Montanans are fortunate to have these options for their winter adventures.
 

Lolo Peak

Aside from the resorts, many back-country adventurers travel about an hour to the Lolo Peak trailhead and make their way through the charred woods towards the glorious Lolo Peak. Missoulians can see the mountain from town and the snowfall stays for many months out of the year. Backcounty can start as early as October and proceed through June. Mountaineer, snow-chasers, and hikers alike can experience the vast nature of the Bitteroot Wilderness, and still be home for dinner!
